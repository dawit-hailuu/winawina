Our Story

The Taste of Addis,
In Every Glass

የሁልጊዜ ምርጫዎ! — your everyday choice. Wina Juice is where Addis Ababa comes for fresh fruit and vegetable juices, smoothies, burgers, pizza, fried chicken and shawarma — all served with our famously warm hospitality.

From Bole Wolo Sefer to Piassa near Cinema Ampir, from Arada to Addisu Gebeya and Summit Fiyel Bet — wherever you are in the city, a glass of Wina freshness is never far away. Our supermarket branches even let you take the freshness home.
